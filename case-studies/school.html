01 / THE CHALLENGE

The Problem

Educational institutions in the region were operating with antiquated systems or no digital presence at all. Parents struggled to find critical information such as admission requirements, fee structures or basic contact details. Furthermore the few existing sites were completely inaccessible on mobile devices which represented 90% of the target audience's traffic.

error No Digital Presence
error Zero Mobile
error Poor Architecture

How I Solved It

01

Strategy

Devised a complete information architecture overhaul. We mapped out school hierarchies to ensure parents find data in under three clicks.

02

Component Design

Developed a library of reusable student portal modules in Figma allowing for rapid deployment while maintaining unique visual branding.

03

Performance

Optimized HTML/CSS/JS builds specifically for schools with limited connectivity ensuring fast loading speeds on 3G networks.
